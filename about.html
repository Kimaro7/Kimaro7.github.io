Lauren Nagel is a Chicago area native with a passion for all things food and cooking. By trade, Lauren is a licensed Family and Consumer Sciences teacher, and spent nearly a decade teaching high school culinary arts. Her passion for cooking and teaching helped inspire the creation of Bon Appeteach in 2018. Lauren was an exclusive partner and brand ambassador with the luxury gas grill brand, Lynx Grills, Primo Ceramic Grills, and a member of the 2020 Big Green Egg professional BBQ team.
